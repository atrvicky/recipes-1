Ingredients:
 - Cocoa 1 bowl
 - sugar as needed
 - fresh cream 2tblspn
 - milk two glasses

Directions:
 1. Shake well, the cream in a mixer.
 2. Add milk to it and shake again.
 3. Now add sugar and heat till foamy.
 4. Add cocoa and stir in simmer.
 5. Refrigerate once completely cooled for 6 hours.
 6. Serve chilled with toppings, if needed.
